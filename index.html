About Us

Casa de Fernandes offers a blend of tradition, elegance, and hospitality in coastal Vagator. Our property is thoughtfully designed for families, couples, and groups seeking comfort and an uplifting Goa vibe.

Stay a short stroll from Vagator Beach, famous shacks, cafes, flea markets, and Goa's nightlife. Every room is beautifully appointed for relaxation and productivity. Discover why our guests come back!

Places Near Me

Fontainhas

Discover the Latin Quarter of Panaji, a UNESCO heritage site famous for its colorful Portuguese houses, street art, and old-world charm.

~19 km from Vagator

Old Goa

Once the capital of Portuguese India, Old Goa is renowned for its majestic cathedrals and basilicas, now part of a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

~23 km from Vagator

Fort Aguada

Iconic 17th-century fort offers sweeping views of the Arabian Sea and a historic lighthouse. Great for sunset photography.

~12 km from Vagator

Thivim Railway Station

Key arrival point for North Goa, connecting major Indian cities. Helpful for travelers coming by train.

~18 km from Vagator

Calangute Beach

The Queen of Beaches, buzzing with water sports, lively shacks, shopping, and nightlife. Great for a day trip.

~9 km from Vagator

Chapora Fort

Famed for its "Dil Chahta Hai" view, this clifftop fort offers panoramic vistas of Vagator's coast. Perfect for sunsets.

~3 km from Vagator

Dabolim Airport (GOI)

Goa's main domestic/international airport, over the Mandovi river. Entry for most flights to Goa.

~42 km from Vagator

Mopa Airport (GOX)

The brand-new Manohar International Airport, North Goa's closest air hub. Fastest way to reach Vagator now.

~27 km from Vagator

Anjuna Flea Market

Every Wednesday, vibrant market for boho fashion, souvenirs, food stalls, and live Goan music.

~6 km from Vagator

Anjuna Beach

Legendary trance and hippie culture, rocky shore, famous nightlife, and relaxed cafes. Lively all day and night.

~5 km from Vagator

Vagator Beach

Dramatic cliffs, red rocks, fresh seafood, and Goa’s most scenic sunsets. Easy walk from Casa de Fernandes.

~1.5 km from Casa de Fernandes
